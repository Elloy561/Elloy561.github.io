missions to guatemala

The Bruins volleyball team will be taking a mission's trip to Guatemala in August of 2023!

About This mission

The Bruins team will be working with Dare for More ministries to minister to women at risk in Guatemala.

The mission of Dare for More Ministries is to rescue and restore women and their families’ lives. Their aim is to equip these women to live a life of dignity and purpose when they come from backgrounds of loss and shame.

We will provide volleyball clinics, play games against local teams, and go into the villages to interact with the women
